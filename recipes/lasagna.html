World's Best Lasagna


Description

This is my all time favourite hearty lasagna dish. Every winter it's choice number one for me and my family. It's a great dish to make for a crowd and easy to adjust for varying sizes of guests. Let's dig into it and get things started!


Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Ground Beef
  • One full onion
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • One can of crushed tomatoes
  • Two cans of tomato sauce
  • Two cans of tomato paste
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Parsley / Basil leaves / salt / Italien seasoning / fennel seeds / black bepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan / Mozzarella / Ricotta cheese
  • One egg

Steps to create lasagna

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
